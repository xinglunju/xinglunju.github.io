Mentor of summer interns at the CfA

I worked with interns at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics in the last three summers. The interns were students from colledge and high schools and spent the summer at the CfA to conduct short-term research projects. I helped them get started with data reduction or programming, and dicussed with them frequently on their work.
2013 summer: with Zoey Bergstrom (Harvard undergraduate), Gas Temperatures in Infrared Dark Clouds Using GBT NH3 Observations
2014 summer: with Juliana Garcia-Mejia (Harvard undergraduate), SMA Observations of Massive Molecular Clouds in the Central Molecular Zone, and William Tan (Needham High School), CSO Multi-Line Mapping of Massive Molecular Clouds in the CMZ.
2015 summer: with Julian Garcia-Mejia (Harvard undergraduate), SMA Observations of Massive Molecular Clouds in the Central Molecular Zone, and Sophie Welsh (Harvard undergraduate), Radio Recombination Line Masers in Star Forming Regions.
