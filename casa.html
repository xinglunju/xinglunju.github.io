General Issues

  • CASA imregrid may introduce a 1-arcsec offset when converting between J2000 and Galactic frames. Use montage_wrapper in astropy!
  • Pay attention to the primary beam shape, especially when mapping a large area outside of the FWHM of the primary beam (e.g., pblimit=0.05 in tclean). This issue may have been fixed in v5.4.1+.
  • For low frequency (e.g., VLA C band) and large mosaics (e.g., more than 10 pointings), enabling w-projection/facets in clean or tclean is necessary, or else phase errors show up for pointings far away from the imaging center and positions of sources are drifted.
  • Combination of ALMA 12m array and ACA data: watch out for the flux consistency?
