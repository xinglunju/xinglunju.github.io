Node: PlaneSensor

X3D: 3.3
Component: PointingDeviceSensor
Status:
experimental

Quick Links

➨ Fields

PlaneSensor converts pointing device motion into 2D translation, parallel to the local Z=0 plane. Hint: You can constrain translation output to one axis by setting the respective minPosition and maxPosition members to equal values for that axis.

HTML Encoding and Default Values⤴

<PlaneSensor autoOffset='true' axisRotation='0,0,1,0' enabled='true' maxPosition='-1,-1' metadata='X3DMetadataObject' minPosition='0,0' offset='0,0,0' planeOrientation=''XY'' ></PlaneSensor>
