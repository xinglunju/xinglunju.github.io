.nodeTypes. CartoonVolumeStyle

The CartoonVolumeStyle node specifies that the associated volume data shall be rendered with a cartoon style non-photorealistic rendering. The cartoon styles uses two colors the rendering will depend on the local surface normals and the view direction.

HTML Encoding and Default Values⤴

<CartoonVolumeStyle colorSteps='4' enabled='true' metadata='X3DMetadataObject' orthogonalColor='1,1,1' parallelColor='0,0,0' surfaceNormals='x3dom.nodeTypes.Texture' ></CartoonVolumeStyle>
