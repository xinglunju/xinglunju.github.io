3D Visualization of CO Outflows in Filamentary Clouds

The CO 2-1 data are obtained with the Submillimeter Array. The 3D iso-surfaces are plotted at the 20-sigma level (1-sigma is 40 mJy/beam for the first 4 sources, and 80 mJy/beam for the next 4 sources). Contours on the back sides of cubes show the SMA 1.3 mm continuum emission, with contour levels of 5, 10, 15, 20, 25, 30, 100, and 150 times sigma levels, where 1-sigma is 1 mJy/beam.
