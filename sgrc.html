3D Visualization of SiO Outflows in the Sgr C cloud

The SiO 5-4 data are obtained with ALMA (Lu et al. 2021).

  • The 3-D contours of the SiO data are plotted at the 3-sigma level, where 1-sigma is 2 mJy/beam. The 3-D contours are color coded, to show the blue and red-shifted gas components.
The interactive modules can be mouse controlled with pan, zoom, and rotate.

The full list of visualizations can be found at this page.

Use the mouse: left click and drag to rotate; right click and drag (or use the middle scroll if you have one) to zoom in/out.
Or use the touchpad moves accordingly.
The code to make the visualization is available at the GitHub page.
